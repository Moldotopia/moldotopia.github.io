Privacy is a love language

Your most intimate data deserves the strongest protection. Here's how INTIMO keeps it safe.

End-to-End Encryption

All sync between paired devices uses AES-256-GCM. Data is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves.

Zero-Knowledge Backend

Our servers relay encrypted data but never see its contents. We can't read your wishes, moods, or messages.

Local-First Architecture

Everything runs on your device first. The app works fully offline, and your data stays on your phone.

Account Optional, No Tracking

Use the app fully without signing up. Optional sign-in unlocks sync and backup. Telemetry is anonymized with personal data stripped.
